Cinema Management System

The Cinema Management System represents a robust database-driven solution developed using SQL, designed to efficiently manage and coordinate the operations of a cinema house chain with multiple branches across Pakistan. This comprehensive system establishes an interconnected web of relational entities, optimizing data storage, retrieval, and reporting functionalities. Among many other things The system maintains individual records for each cinema branch, including details such as branch name, location, seating capacity, and contact information.It also keeps tracks of movies, screenings, customers,earnings etc.
